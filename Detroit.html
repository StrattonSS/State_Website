Welcome to Detroit, Michigan

Detroit, the Motor City, pulses with a rhythm all its own—bold, soulful, and unstoppable. As the birthplace of Motown and the heart of America’s automotive legacy, Detroit is a city built on ingenuity and grit. Today, it’s experiencing a remarkable renaissance, where historic architecture meets cutting-edge innovation. From world-class museums and live music venues to passionate sports fans and diverse neighborhoods, Detroit offers a dynamic blend of culture, pride, and progress that continues to shape its powerful identity.

Top 5 Things to Do in Detroit

  1. Tour the Motown Museum, where music legends got their start.
  2. Explore the Detroit Institute of Arts, home to the famous Rivera murals.
  3. Walk the revitalized Detroit Riverwalk and enjoy skyline views.
  4. Catch a Tigers, Lions, Pistons, or Red Wings game downtown.
  5. Shop and dine at Eastern Market, one of the oldest markets in the U.S.

Did you know?

  • Detroit is the birthplace of the American automotive industry.
  • The city’s nickname "Motown" comes from “Motor Town.”
  • Detroit is one of the few U.S. cities where you can look south into Canada.
